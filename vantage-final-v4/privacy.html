Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What we collect

Vantage AI collects API usage metadata (token counts, model names, latency, cost estimates) that you send through the SDK. We do not store the content of your prompts or responses by default. Only statistical event data is retained.

2. How we use data

Usage data is used solely to provide the Vantage dashboard — cost tracking, analytics, and alerts. We do not sell data to third parties or use it to train AI models.

3. Data storage

Events are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You may delete your data at any time from the dashboard settings.

4. Authentication

We use Supabase Auth with optional Google OAuth. We store your email address for account identification. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

5. Cookies

Vantage uses localStorage and sessionStorage for authentication tokens and dashboard preferences. No third-party advertising cookies are set.

6. Data retention

Events are retained per your plan: Free (30 days), Starter (90 days), Team (1 year), Enterprise (custom). You may export or delete your data at any time.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/Amanjain98/VantageAI.
